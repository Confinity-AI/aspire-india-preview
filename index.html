Why it works

Our job is to help a whole village make sure every child learnsevery child learns — and to keep it going long after we've moved on.

We get children into school, help them learn once they're there, and hand the running of it to local people — in some of the hardest places in India to reach.
What we do

Three things, all at once

01

Access

We find children who are out of school and help them get back — and stay to the end.
02

Learning

We help children who have fallen behind catch up, and learn to keep learning on their own.
03

Governance

We help parents, schools, and village councils run and protect education themselves.
